Kerala - God's Own Country

Previous Next
A Beautiful City situated in the lap of green environment and water-bodies. Memories of Kerala's Scenary remains forever in people's mind once visited.
Kerala is blessed with misty hill stations, green valleys, refreshing waterfalls, numerous rivers, exotic flora and fauna which remains evergreen.
It's beauty attracts travellers not only from India but from all over the world. It is one of the most sought tourist space.
People who visited Kerala takes back its peaceful memories and refresing mind and soul. Kerala offers a true feel of nature at its best.
From the tourism point of view, travellers in Kerala could do outdoor activities such as Swimming, sea-surfing, and water sporting along beaches.
People must visit Kerala once in thier life so that they can enjoy that beauty.

Seasons Of Kerala.

Kerala has a pleasant and equable climate throughout the year. The seasons in kerala that are generally experienced are Monsoons( June- September and October-November) and Summer(Feburary- May). Slight drop in temperature in Kerala from the normal range of 28-32 degree celcius causes Winter. Pleasant temperature in Kerala attracts most of the tourist.
